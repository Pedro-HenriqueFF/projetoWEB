Hu Tao/Xingqiu/C4 Yanfei/Sucrose



Pyro VV Vape Variant
shield ✅ healing ✅ low investment ✅

Pros & Cons
+ Yanfei uses essentially the same artifacts as Thoma but requires no talent investment
+ her C4 shield is strong, starts fully generated and lasts long enough for almost full team coverage
+ prototype amber is f2p, lowers energy requirements and provides small healing
+ she has multiple ways to apply pyro for swirling, recommended orders to use these are mentioned below
+ Yanfei can use any of these pyro catalyst attacks to reapply pyro if a rotation goes wrong

- unlike Thoma has no constellations to lower energy requirements
- unrealistic to give her 4pc instructors for increased damage, needs very good artifacts
Potential Ease of Play Issues
- pyro application is ideally applied from slight range so can be annoying with enemies closing the gaps
- r5 prototype amber can over heal Hu Tao in certain situations
- vv vape teams have long setups; takes time to start attacking with Hu Tao
- ineffective vs enemies with innate elemental auras e.g slimes
Builds Example (er requirements range from general mob content to bossing)
Hu Tao, 4pc CW or 4pc SR
C4 Yanfei, r3 prototype amber, 4pc noblesse oblige 210-220% ER (ER reqs go higher/lower with refines)
Sucrose, r5 ttds, 4pc viridescent venerer
Xingqiu, 4pc emblem of severed fate or mixed set, 180-190% ER

Which Weapon
prototype amber: build er/hp/hp
fav codex: build hp/hp/cr, (do e + q + ca to catch fav particles!)
prioritise er substats with both

Rotation Example (21 seconds)
xingqiu e + q + n1 > yanfei e + ca + q (if wearing noblesse) yanfei e + q + ca (if wearing mixed artifacts or using fav codex) > sucrose n3 + e > hu tao combos > repeat
